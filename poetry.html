Ode to The Difference Engine

Oh Difference Engine, you were made in 1822
What would I ever do without you?

Oh Difference Engine, you were made by Mr. Charles Babbage
I would call you a calculation savage

You were made to autonomate calculations
Used to solve mathmatical equations

Once the Difference Engine came
Then arrived the World Wide Web's fame

Oh Difference Engine, we thank you
Oh Difference Engine, where would we be without you?
